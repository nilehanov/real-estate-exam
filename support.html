Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the app?

Select a category from the home screen and start answering practice questions. You can switch between Multiple Choice and Case Study modes using the tabs. Track your progress with the stats bar at the top of each category.

What is included for free?

All 1,000 multiple choice questions across 13 categories are completely free. No account or subscription required.

What does Premium include?

Premium unlocks 100 real-world Case Study scenarios (300 questions) and detailed explanations for all 1,000 multiple choice questions. Premium is available for $0.99/month with a 1-week free trial.

How do I restore a previous purchase?

Open the app, tap any locked content to open the paywall, and tap "Restore Purchases." Your subscription will be restored if it is still active.

Do I need an internet connection?

No. All question content is stored locally on your device. An internet connection is only needed for purchasing or restoring a subscription.

Manage Your Subscription

To view, change, or cancel your subscription:

  • Open Settings on your iPhone or iPad
  • Tap your name (Apple ID) at the top
  • Tap Subscriptions
  • Select Real Estate Exam Preps
  • Choose a new plan or tap Cancel Subscription

If you cancel during the free trial, you will not be charged. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.
