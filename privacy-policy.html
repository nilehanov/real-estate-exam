Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Overview

Real Estate Exam ("the App") is a study tool for real estate exam preparation. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All quiz progress and scores are stored locally on your device only and are never sent to any server.

No Analytics or Tracking

The App does not use any analytics services, advertising frameworks, or tracking technologies. We do not track your usage, location, or any other personal information.

No Account Required

The App does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use it.

Purchases & Subscriptions

The App offers an optional auto-renewing subscription managed through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information. All purchase transactions are handled by Apple. We may receive anonymized transaction confirmation data from Apple to verify your subscription status, but we do not have access to your payment details. Apple's privacy policy governs how Apple handles purchase-related data.

Third-Party Services

The App uses Apple's StoreKit framework to manage optional in-app subscriptions. Apple's privacy policy applies to purchase-related data. All fonts are self-hosted and served locally; no external font services are used. No other third-party services that collect user data are integrated.

Data Storage

Subscription status and any preferences or progress data are stored exclusively on your device using local storage. This data is not accessible to us or any third party and is not transmitted to any server.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children. The App is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
